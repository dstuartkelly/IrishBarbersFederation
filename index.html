The Barbers Federation of Ireland is revolutionizing the barbering industry through professional recognition, training, and advocacy.

~16,000 Practicing Barbers in Ireland

Our goal is to help anyone wishing to become a professional barber as a trade get the relevant trainnig, tools and licencing needed to do so.

Our industry in the Republic of Ireland is unregulated. Essentially, anybody can pick up clippers and scissors and call themselves a barber with no formal training. This dilutes the hard work and effort of people who choose barbering as a career.
