Welcome to the Barbers Federation of Ireland Website

From the 2022 Census carried out by the Central Statistics Office we estimate there are approximately 16,000 practicing barbers in Ireland.

Our goal is to help anyone wishing to become a professional barber as a trade get the relevant trainnig, tools and licencing needed to do so.

Our industry, in the Republic of Ireland, is unregulated. Its a free for all. Essentially, anybody can pick up a clippers and a scissors and call themselves a barber with no formal training. This dilutes the hard work and effort that people who choose to have barbering as a career as opposed to a quick money maker.

This website aims to be a portal where Barbers can register and become licenced.

The Barbers Federation of Ireland will unite Barbers, Shop Owners and Educators to elevate the barbering industry.
