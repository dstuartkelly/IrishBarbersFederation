The Barbers Federation of Ireland.

Founded in 2025, the Barbers Federation of Ireland is a non profit organisation dedicated to supporting members in the barbering industry by providing:

  • A central register of all Barbers working in Ireland.
  • Training and Certification.
  • Recognition of time spent working.
  • Guidance for new barbers.
  • Certification of skills.
  • Support to shops.
  • Representation to local Government.

We are here to provide support, and representation to the members of the Barbers Federation across the Barbering industry.

The goal of the Barbers Federation of Ireland is to recognise and protect Barbers skills and training.
